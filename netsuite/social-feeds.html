Social Product Feeds

    Contents

    Updated: September 24, 2021

    Some helpful references for adding Netsuite Product feeds to social platforms.

    Generating feeds:

    Login to netsuite -> Lists -> Feeds -> Product Feeds -> New

    Facebook and Google

    Both the Facebook and Google feeds are pretty straight forward. Netsuite has an option that generates both feeds with the required fields and information already needed for both platforms.
